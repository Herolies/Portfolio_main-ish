Hi, I'm Andres, Miami-raised, FIU Computer Science grad, and someone who genuinely loves the intersection of technology and creativity. I picked up photography in August 2024 and haven't put the camera down since. There's something about framing a shot that feels a lot like debugging, you keep adjusting until it just clicks.

By day I'm a Shift Lead at Best Buy, where I lead teams, track metrics, and make sure everything runs smoothly. Before that I worked as a Data Analyst at Appen and even helped build ShellHacks, the largest hackathon in Florida, as part of Upsilon Pi Epsilon's tech committee.

When I'm not shooting or coding, I'm deep in a game, watching films, or picking apart whatever new tech just dropped. English and Spanish, always.
